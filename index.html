Development Achievements

I analyzed, designed, developed, and maintained the subsystems of Purchasing, Stock, Inventory, and Medical Fees.
The subsystems of Purchasing, Stock, and Inventory saved thousands of Reals (Brazilian currency) because of the stock control. In other words, all items are controlled and tracked into the hospital. A special note for the items near the due date that generate alerts and reports.

Purchasing

This subsystem creates quotes, analyzes the previous quotes, suggests the cheaper provider, and emits the invoice for three categories of materials Orthosis and Prosthesis, Medicaments, and Consume. In addition, it generates many reports for analysis and audit.

Inventory

This subsystem creates the controls, entries and reports for an inventory process performed by the audit team.

Stock

This subsystem takes care of stock entry and tracks the movements of each item inside of the hospital. There are reports for items consumed, items near the due date, and audit.

Import

The quotes for this subsystem is similar to the Purchasing subsystem. However, the data and the product differs from a normal purchase. Moreover, there is a different process of transportation and specific documents in a foreign language, for example, international insurance.
